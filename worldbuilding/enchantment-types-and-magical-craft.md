## Overview

This document defines the taxonomy, mechanics, and commercial applications of enchantments in Nubimancy. Delyra's Art of Facts is built on a central belief: magic should be trustworthy, affordable, and useful to ordinary people â€” not just those wealthy enough to afford a wizard.

Because enchantment is **item-based magic** (not personal spellcasting), anyone can benefit from an enchanted item without magical ability of their own. This is its great democratizing power. A farmer's family can have a freshness charm on their root cellar. A blacksmith can have an ever-sharp chisel. A caravan guard can have a ward on their shield. Delyra's mission is to ensure those items are genuine, properly made, and reasonably priced â€” and that the post-plague charlatan economy doesn't poison that trust.

Understanding enchantment types, decay rates, and refresh methods is essential for demo data generation across the Delyra extension group and for cross-hero dependencies (ale preservation for Thorin, scroll integrity for Bran, arena equipment for Rini, component sourcing for Weltina).

## Enchantment Lifecycle and Decay

### Decay Mechanics

All enchantments decay over time. Decay rate reflects the gradual dissipation of the magical binding that holds the enchantment to the item. It is neither a flaw nor a failure â€” it is the natural behaviour of enchantment, and the principle on which Art of Facts' refresh service business is built.

**Base lifespan by tier (undisturbed, ideal conditions):**

| Tier | Base Lifespan Range | Notes |
|------|--------------------|----|
| Tier 1 | 30â€“90 days | Varies significantly by enchantment type and material substrate |
| Tier 2 | 90â€“365 days | Quality materials and correct lunar timing push toward upper end |
| Tier 3 | 1â€“3 years | Delyra's craftsmanship reliably reaches full range |
| Tier 4 | Indefinite | Degrade only if not maintained on schedule; Delyra maintains personally |

**Decay rate accelerants:**
- **Environmental exposure:** Heat, cold extremes, damp, and direct magical interference all accelerate decay; preservation enchantments on items outdoors or in harsh conditions decay 20â€“40% faster
- **Usage intensity:** Actively-used items (fighter wards in combat, tool enhancements in daily use) decay faster than passive items (a stored freshness charm on a sealed larder)
- **Material incompatibility:** Inferior base materials (low-quality iron, softwood, impure glass) hold enchantment less well; Tier 1 applied to quality materials may outlast Tier 1 on cheap materials by 50%
- **Proximity to the Conjunction:** Enchantments applied in the week before the Conjunction show slightly accelerated early decay; Art of Facts advises refreshing critical items after the Conjunction period rather than before

## Refresh and Renewal Services

Refresh services are Art of Facts' most reliable recurring revenue stream and the service Delyra personally considers most important. A refreshed enchantment is not re-enchanted from scratch â€” the existing magical binding is reinforced and extended, costing less in materials and time than a new item while restoring the item to Full health.

### Standard Refresh Methods

**Method A: In-Situ Refresh (On-Site)**  
A journeyman or master enchanter travels to the client's location and refreshes the item in place. Preferred for large, immovable items (storage rooms, building wards, arena installations).  
- Works for: Tier 1â€“3 preservation, warding, enhancement  
- Cost: Base refresh fee + travel cost (varies by distance from nearest shop)  
- Lead time: 3â€“10 days for standard scheduling  
- Limitation: Cannot achieve full Dual-Moon timing benefit in the field

**Method B: Workshop Refresh**  
Client brings the item to any Art of Facts shop location for workshop processing. Preferred for valuable objects where optimal lunar timing is worth waiting for.  
- Works for: All tiers  
- Cost: Base refresh fee only (no travel surcharge)  
- Lead time: 2â€“14 days depending on tier and lunar scheduling  
- Advantage: Restores to 100% health; can incorporate material upgrades; extends base lifespan by 10â€“20% over in-situ  
- Process: Item is assessed on arrival, logged in BC, processed in the pocket dimension workshop, returned via portal or pickup

**Method C: Remote Scrying-Assisted Refresh**  
Delyra or a senior journeyman provides guided refresh instructions via scrying crystal while the client performs minor steps. Only viable for Tier 1 items with a moderately skilled operator.
